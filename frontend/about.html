About PhishGuard

PhishGuard is an AI-powered platform dedicated to protecting users from digital scams, phishing emails, and malicious URLs. Our mission is to provide easy-to-use tools that empower individuals to stay safe in an increasingly complex digital world.


Using state-of-the-art machine learning models, we analyze text patterns and URL features to detect threats in real-time. Whether it's a suspicious SMS about a bank account or a phishing email claiming you've won a lottery, PhishGuard is here to help you identify the risk before it's too late.
