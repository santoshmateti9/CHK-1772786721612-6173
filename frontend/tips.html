Cyber Safety Tips

1. Never share OTP

One-Time Passwords (OTPs) are for your eyes only. Banks or official services will never ask for them over a call or message.

2. Verify Website Domains

Always check the URL. Phishing sites often use slightly misspelled names (e.g., faceboook.com instead of facebook.com).

3. Avoid Clicking Unknown Links

If you receive an unexpected message with a link, don't click it. Manually type the website address in your browser.

4. Use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Enable 2FA on all your important accounts to add an extra layer of security beyond just a password.
